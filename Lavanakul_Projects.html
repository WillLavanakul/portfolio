Principles of Engineering

Mechanical Winch Project 10/14/16-10/23/16

The objective of this project was to build an efficient mechanism that must lift at least 100 grams over at least 30 cm over the side of a table. My specific role in the project was to build the machine, revise documentation, and help with the Netlogo simulation. A problem we encountered while building the machine was that it was too unstable. We designed 2 side pillars that would stabilize the machine. Another problem we ran into was that our resulting gear ratio was about 1:4, which meant that the driven gear was spinning 4 times faster than the driving gear. However, when we tested the winch, it was too weak and did not move. We then moved on to change the gear ratio into having more torque, and testing which was better. We realized that the winch failed to lift the weight not because of the lack of torque, but that the gears were not meshing properly, and they were forced together because of the failure of the Fischertechnik pieces on the stand to exactly accommodate the gear size. To solve this, we chained the last two gears together and changed the gear train to higher torque. There was a great deal in teamwork. Many days we would find ourselves having 3 of the 4 group members present. This would mean we would have to work harder and more efficiently. I paired up with another team member to fix all the problems with the machine along with working on the NetLogo program.

Entrepreneurial Project 5/4/18-5/25/18

In this project, we used Python to create an app that allows the user to summarize YouTUbe videos into short and concise text based notes. To do this, we first get the caption track from the video. This caption track has the captions, but it also has time stamps and line numbers. We created 2 different functions that sort through the captions and removes all the time stamps and the line numbers. It then becomes just the actual transcript for the video. From this transcript, we are able to make an algorithm that runs through the transcript and takes out the main points printing them to a document. This solution saves time for the user because they do not have to watch extremely long videos to figure out the most important information needed. Also, understanding deeply the main points of an educational video is better than just knowing briefly the whole video overall. Not only does this help save time, it also allows the reader to learn the necessary material more efficiently. If they are extremely busy, then they could just use this app and it would save them upwards of an hour. This app saves time and gives the user a better education. It can be used for any video found on YouTube so it is also, a very versatile solution. We believe that this is the best solution to the problem because there are no downsides to this solution. In addition, there are numerous benefits that are created through this solution. In addition, the user is able to decide to what extent the YouTube video is summarized by specifying a percentage.
