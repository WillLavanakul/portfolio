Sometimes I find myself reading up on infornation regarding computer technology and even sometimes exploring different builds for computers.

This is true for a lot of the things that I do in life: projects, gymnastics, art, curiosity over computers, and the sorts. This is extremely useful when debugging code and working with finer details of technology. Regardless of having the solution or not, identifying the problem is the first step of fixing something.
